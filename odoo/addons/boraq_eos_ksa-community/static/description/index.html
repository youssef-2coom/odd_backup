End of Service

The aim of this module is to calculate the end of service reward, which is given to the employee at the last pay slip generated for him/her. The calculation of this reward is based on Saudi labor law.

    The reward is calculated under three options:

  • 1- If the employee signs for his/her resignation

    • If the employee spends less than 2 years in the company then he/she wouldn't get any reward.

    • If the employee spends between 2 and 5 years in the company then he/she will:
      Reward = ((Wage/2) * service years)/3

    • If the employee spends between 5 and 10 years then his/her reward will be:
      Reward = ((Wage*2.5) +(Wage*(service years - 5)) *(2/3)

    • If the employee spends more than 10 years:
      Reward = (Wage*2.5) + (Wage *(service years - 5))

  • 2- If the contract ends and both sides (company and employee) don't need to renew it

    • If the employee spends less than 2 years in the company then he/she wouldn't get anything.

    • If the employee spends between 2 and 5 years in the company then he/she will:
      Reward = (Wage/2) * service years

    • If the employee spends between 5 and 10 years then his/her reward will be:
      Reward = ((Wage*2.5) +(Wage*(service years - 5))

    • If the employee spends more than 10 years:
      Reward = (Wage*2.5) + (Wage *(service years - 5))

      Reward = (Wage/2)*5 + (Wage*(service year - 5))

Configuration

  • 1- From payroll module go to Configuration menu, then choose "Salary Structure"
  • 2- Choose any structure from the list
  • 3- Click on "Edit" button
  • 4- Click on Add a line, then choose "Reward" from the options
  • 5- The reward will be added as a line in salary rules. Just click "Save" button

Usage

    In order to get the End of service reward, we have to go through the following steps:

  • 1- Go to employee contract, navigate to "EoS Setup tab" then activate "End of Contract?" option

    Then choose reason for ending the contract:

    • Resign
    • End of contract
    • Terminate
  • 2- Go to payroll, click on create new Employee Payslip

  • 3- Fill the pay slip information, and then click on "Compute Sheet"
    Then you will get the result
