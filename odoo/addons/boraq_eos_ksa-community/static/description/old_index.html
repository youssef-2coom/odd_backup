End of Service

The aim of this module is to calculate the end of service reward, which is given to the employee at the last pay slip generated for him. The calculation of this reward is based on Saudi labor law

The reward is calculated under three options:

1- If the employee sign for his resignation
  • If the employee spends less than 2 years in the company then he wouldn’t get any reward.
  • If the employee spends between 2 and 5 years in the company then he will:
    Reward = ((Wage/2) * service years)/3
  • If the employee spends between 5 and 10 years then his reward will be:
    Reward = ((Wage*2.5) +(Wage*(service years – 5)) *(2/3)
  • If the employee spends more than 10 years:
    Reward = (Wage*2.5) + (Wage *(service years – 5))
2- If the contract is end and both sides (company and employee) don’t need to renew it
  • If the employee spends less than 2 years in the company then he wouldn’t get anything.
  • b. If the employee spends between 2 and 5 years in the company then he will:
    Reward = (Wage/2) * service years
  • If the employee spends between 5 and 10 years then his reward will be:
    Reward = ((Wage*2.5) +(Wage*(service years – 5))
  • d. If the employee spends more than 10 years:
    Reward = (Wage*2.5) + (Wage *(service years – 5))
3- 3. If the company terminates employee’s contract
  • If the employee spends more than 10 years he will get:
    Reward = (Wage/2)*5 + (Wage*(service year – 5))
Configuration

1- From payroll module go to Configuration menu, then choose Salary Structure



2- Choose any structure from the list



3- Click on Edit Button



4- Click on Add a line, then choose "Reward" from the options



5- The reward will be added as a line in salary rules. Just click “Save” button



Usage

In order to get the End of service reward, we have to go through the following steps:

1- Go to employee contract, navigate to “EoS Setup tab” then activate “End of Contract?” option

Then choose reason for ending the contract:

  • Resign
  • End of contract
  • Terminate


2- Go to payroll, click on create new Employee Payslip



3- Fill the pay slip information, and then click on “Compute Sheet”

Then you will get the result
